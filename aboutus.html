About us

At Carlos Embellishers, our primary mission is to fulfill the embellishment needs of esteemed apparel brands and leading manufacturers in Sri Lanka. We are dedicated to introducing innovative concepts that revolutionize the embellishment industry.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our close working relationships and strategic proximity to our valued customers. With seven state-of-the-art production facilities located in Veyangoda, Katunayake, Horana, Trincomalee, Welioya, Kekirawa, and Malwana, we are well-positioned to serve your needs efficiently and effectively.

We specialize in a wide range of embellishment products, from the simple to the sophisticated, continuously innovating to bring fresh techniques and trends to the market. Our relentless pursuit of innovation and quality ensures that we consistently deliver products that set new standards in the industry.

At Carlos Embellishers, we are not just participants in the embellishment sector; we are pioneers, driving change and redefining what it means to be an embellisher. Join us as we transform the traditional perspective and make a lasting impact on the world of apparel embellishment.


Timeline of Growth and Innovation

2011 September 29

Inception and Initial Steps

Our journey began with 20 visionary colleagues, embarking on a mission to revolutionize screen printing in the textile industry. From humble beginnings, we set our sights on creating exceptional prints.

Late 2012

Embracing New Technologies

With innovation at our core, we transitioned to sublimation printing, unlocking new possibilities and expanding our creative horizons. This leap forward marked the dawn of a new era for our company.

2014 December

Strategic Expansion

Our expansion to Katunayake was a pivotal moment. By launching screen printing services within the Crystal Martin Europe Katunayake factory, we cemented our presence in a key industrial hub, poised for further growth.

2016 January

Scaling New Heights

Our ambitions soared as we scaled new heights by extending our production capacity to BOI EPZ Horana. This significant move enabled us to meet increasing demand and set new benchmarks in the industry.

2019 January

Diversification and New Ventures

We ventured into Heat Seal Transfer Printing at our primary facility in Veyangoda, pushing the boundaries of our expertise. Simultaneously, we inaugurated a new facility in the Trincomalee Industrial Zone, marking a year of bold diversification.

2021 January

Broadening Horizons

Expanding our production footprint to the Welioya Industrial Zone was a strategic decision that broadened our horizons and enhanced our regional influence. This move fortified our commitment to growth and excellence.

2022 August

Strengthening Partnerships and Capabilities

We bolstered our production capabilities by expanding within Rainbow Exports Kekirawa. In December, a strategic collaboration with Norlanka at their Malwana fabric-cutting facility marked a new chapter in our partnership-driven approach.

2023 October

Major Milestones and Innovations

The unveiling of our state-of-the-art plant on 3.5 acres in Veyangoda was a crowning achievement. The DOD & Adhesive Bonding Project, housed in this cutting-edge facility, addressed burgeoning customer demands and underscored our innovative spirit.

2024 May

Technological Advancements

The introduction of Galvo & Flat Bed Laser Machines signaled a leap into the future, offering complimentary services that enhanced our technological prowess. This advancement epitomized our commitment to staying at the forefront of industry innovation.
