Our Locations

 


In a lush green garden of 05 acres, Head Office and Main Factory is located in Gampaha District and addressed at Dambuwa Estate, Dadagamuwa, Veyangoda within 2.6 km along the Kalagedihena (Kandy Road) to Veyangoda Road. The facility has 40,000 bult up factory and building space over 05 acres of greenland. We will make a green Production Facility. Industrial pollution and waste are minimized through use of clean technology, waste management and policies for environment preservation. We will work towards Green Certification of the factory at International Standards. Highest ethical and regulatory compliances are in practice, and we serve our customers in compliane to highest local and global standards.
