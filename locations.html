Island Wide Our Network

In a green lush garden of 05 acres, Head Office and Main Factory are located in Gampaha District and addressed at Dambuwa Estate, Dadagamuwa, Veyangoda within 2.6 km along the Kalagedihena ( Kandy Road ) to Veyangoda Road.
The facility has 40,000 built factories and building space over 05 acres of Greenland.
We will make a green Production Facility.

Industrial pollution and waste are minimized through the use of clean technology, waste management, and policies for environmental preservation.
We will work towards the Green Certification of the factory at International Standards. The highest ethical and regulatory compliances are in practice and we serve our customers in compliance with highest local and global standards
